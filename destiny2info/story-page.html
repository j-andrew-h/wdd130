The story begins in Destiny 1, where the Guardians fight against various alien races that threaten the Last City, the last bastion of humanity on Earth. The Guardians also explore the secrets of the Traveler and its ancient enemies, such as the Vex, a race of time-traveling machines, and the Hive, a horde of undead creatures that worship the Darkness. The Guardians face off against powerful foes such as Crota, the son of Oryx, the Taken King, who invades the solar system with his army of corrupted beings; Atheon, the leader of the Vex in the Vault of Glass, a mysterious realm outside of time; and Skolas, a rebel Fallen leader who tries to unite his people under his banner.

The story continues in Destiny 2, where the Guardians lose their connection to the Traveler after a surprise attack by Ghaul, the leader of the Red Legion, a faction of the Cabal, a militaristic empire. The Guardians must reclaim their Light and fight back against Ghaul and his forces, while also dealing with other threats such as Calus, the exiled emperor of the Cabal who invites them to his Leviathan ship; Panoptes, a Vex mind that tries to manipulate reality in the Infinite Forest, a simulation engine on Mercury; Xol, a Worm God of the Hive that awakens on Mars; and Riven, a Taken Ahamkara, a wish-granting dragon that corrupts the Dreaming City, a sacred place for the Awoken race.

The story expands in Destiny 2: Forsaken, where the Guardians seek revenge for the death of Cayde-6, a beloved Vanguard leader who was killed by Uldren Sov, the prince of the Awoken who was manipulated by Riven. The Guardians hunt down Uldren and his Scorned Barons in the Tangled Shore, a lawless region in the Reef. They also discover the secrets of the Dreaming City and its curse cycle, which is caused by Riven's last wish and Savathun, Oryx's sister and the Witch Queen of the Hive. The Guardians also face new challenges such as Siviks, a Fallen black marketeer who steals powerful weapons from the Black Armory; The Drifter, a rogue Lightbearer who runs a dangerous game called Gambit; and Gahlran, a Cabal warlord who is corrupted by an artifact from Calus.

The story evolves in Destiny 2: Shadowkeep, where the Guardians return to the Moon after discovering a mysterious disturbance. They encounter Eris Morn, a former Guardian who has been haunted by nightmares of her past. They also discover a massive pyramid ship buried under the surface of the Moon, which is revealed to be one of many vessels of the Darkness that have been dormant for centuries. The Guardians must confront their fears and nightmares while also dealing with new enemies such as Hashladun, a daughter of Crota who seeks to resurrect her father; The Undying Mind, a Vex mind that tries to alter the timeline in favor of the Darkness; and The Sanctified Mind, a Vex mind that has integrated with a pyramid ship in the Garden of Salvation.

The story progresses in Destiny 2: Beyond Light, where the Guardians travel to Europa, a frozen moon of Jupiter that harbors secrets from humanity's Golden Age. They encounter Variks, a former Fallen ally who has sparked a civil war among his people; Eramis, a Fallen leader who has embraced Stasis, a new power derived from the Darkness; and The Exo Stranger, a mysterious time traveler who warns them about an impending threat. The Guardians must decide whether to use Stasis or not while also facing new foes such as Taniks, an infamous Fallen mercenary who has become an abomination; Atraks-1, an Exo Fallen who has infiltrated the Deep Stone Crypt; and The Kell of Darkness, Eramis's final form.

In The Witch Queen, the player confronts Savathun, the cunning sister of Oryx, the Taken King. She has been manipulating events behind the scenes for a long time, and now seeks to unleash her final plan. The player must enter her throne world and stop her from becoming immortal.
